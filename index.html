Tulsa Heating and Air Conditioning Pros

Wortman Central Air has been operating in the Tulsa area since 1968 and has an excellent reputation as Tulsa's premier source for Tulsa Heating and Air Conditioning needs. Bob and Dana Enochs, the owners of Wortman, have been licensed Mechanical Contractor and Advanced HVAC/R Instructors for over 10 years. Bob works closely with all of his technicians and you will often find him out in the field working right along side his crews, and Dana brings her technical and accounting experiencing to managing the office operations.

At Wortman Central Air, we offer NATE Certified Technicians, and we're also the only Building Performance Institute Goldstar Certified Contractor in the state of Oklahoma. In other words, Wortman Central Air has the experience, the expertise, and the leadership to give you incredible service for your Heating and Cooling needs in Tulsa, and throughout the state of Oklahoma.

Wortman Central Air has teamed up with PSO and ONG to offer exciting incentives for upgrading the efficiency of your equipment and to dramatically improve the efficiency of your home. In addition, we have a Building Performance Institute Certified Building Analyst on staff ready to help you improve the efficiency of your home and the quality of the air you breathe.

We are a PSO Certified Contractor for the Home Performance with ENERGY STAR Program and are able to offer you exciting and substantial rebates. When coupled with ONG and Manufacturer rebates, the savings are truly astounding. It's just another way that we're taking Tulsa Heating and Air Conditioning solutions to a whole new level.

We service most of northeastern Oklahoma. That includes Tulsa, Broken Arrow, Owasso, Bixby, Sapulpa, Jenks and more!

Call us today, and discover how Wortman Central Air has earned our amazing reputation in Tulsa. We'd love to solve your HVAC needs, too!

Fast Reliable Service

Wortman Central Air Conditioning provides fast, reliable, and dependable Tulsa heating and air conditioning service to Tulsa and surrounding areas. If your system needs service, we can help!

Service & Replacement

Our NATE-certified technicians are highly trained experts in HVAC/R service and replacements. We guarantee to do the job right the first time- no matter how complex.

Honest and Fair
